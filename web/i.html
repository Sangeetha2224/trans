Our Services

MILKRUN

ST Logistics provides Milk Run services to major customers across Coimbatore, operating over 55 owned vehicles. We cover more than 5,000 km daily, serving 300+ suppliers with reliable delivery and pickup. With 65+ dedicated drivers working around the clock and our trusted workshop for vehicle maintenance, we ensure on-time performance every day.

Full Truck Load (FTL)

ST Logistics offers efficient and cost-effective Full Truck Load (FTL) services because of own fleets for businesses with large shipment needs. With our fleet of 55+ vehicles, we provide direct point-to-point delivery, ensuring faster transit times, minimal handling, and maximum security for your goods. Ideal for bulk cargo or time-sensitive deliveries, our FTL services are trusted by major companies across Coimbatore.
